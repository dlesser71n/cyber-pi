## Executive Summary

cyber-pi + TQAKB-v3.3 occupies a unique market position: **Industry-Specific Threat Intelligence + Strategic Advisory** at mid-market pricing.

**Key Finding:** No competitor offers this combination at our price point ($5K-25K/year vs $30K-150K+ for alternatives).

## 1. Recorded Future - Primary Enterprise Competitor

### Company Profile
- **Founded:** 2009
- **Revenue:** $300M+ annually  
- **Customers:** 1,400+ (40% Fortune 1000)
- **Pricing:** $50K-150K/year

### What They Offer
- 1,000+ threat intelligence sources
- Dark web monitoring
- Vulnerability intelligence
- Brand protection
- Third-party risk scoring
- SIEM/SOAR integrations

### Their Strengths
✅ Market leader brand recognition  
✅ Massive source coverage (1,000+)  
✅ 15 years of historical data  
✅ Large analyst team  
✅ Extensive integrations  
✅ Fortune 100 customer base

### Their Weaknesses
❌ Generic industry coverage (one-size-fits-all)  
❌ No strategic advisory or solution mapping  
❌ Expensive ($50K-150K/year)  
❌ Complex platform, steep learning curve  
❌ Data overload (too much noise)  
❌ No semantic AI or relationship graphing

### How We Win Against Them

**Cost:** 85-95% savings ($5K-25K vs $50K-150K)

**Customization:** 18 deep industry verticals vs generic coverage

**Strategic Value:** Gartner solution mapping, budget roadmaps, quick wins guides

**Technology:** Semantic AI (TQAKB), relationship graphs (Neo4j), 5ms response times

**Clarity:** Focused, actionable reports vs data dumps

### Sales Positioning vs Recorded Future

**Our Message:**  
*"Recorded Future provides data. We provide decisions. Get 80% of the intelligence value at 15% of the cost, plus strategic guidance they don't offer."*

**Win Scenarios:**
- Price-sensitive enterprises
- Companies wanting strategic guidance
- Buyers frustrated with Recorded Future complexity
- Mid-market (Fortune 500-1000)

**Lose Scenarios:**
- Fortune 100 needing "best in class" brand
- Need for 1,000+ sources
- 15+ years historical data required
- Risk-averse buyers (want established vendor)

## 3. ThreatConnect - Closest Direct Competitor ⚠️

### Company Profile
- **Founded:** 2011
- **Revenue:** $50M annually
- **Customers:** 1,000+ mid-market
- **Pricing:** $30K-100K/year

### What They Offer
- Threat Intelligence Platform (TIP)
- SOAR automation capabilities
- Playbook orchestration
- Multi-source aggregation (500+ feeds)
- Community intelligence sharing
- CAL™ analytics language
- Industry "packages"

### Feature-by-Feature Comparison

| Capability | ThreatConnect | cyber-pi + TQAKB | Winner |
|------------|---------------|------------------|--------|
| **Source Count** | 500+ | 80 curated | ThreatConnect |
| **Industry Depth** | Surface packages | 18 deep verticals | **Us** |
| **Semantic AI** | Basic NLP | GPU vector search | **Us** |
| **Relationship Graphs** | Limited | Full Neo4j | **Us** |
| **Strategic Advisory** | ❌ None | ✅ Gartner mapping | **Us** |
| **Budget Planning** | ❌ None | ✅ Roadmaps | **Us** |
| **Quick Wins Guides** | ❌ None | ✅ 30-day plans | **Us** |
| **SOAR Integration** | ✅ Native | ⚠️ API only | ThreatConnect |
| **Price** | $30K-100K | $5K-25K | **Us** |
| **Community** | ✅ Large | ❌ Small | ThreatConnect |
| **Market Maturity** | 13 years | New | ThreatConnect |
| **Complexity** | High (platform) | Low (reports) | **Us** |

### Key Differentiation

**ThreatConnect =** Threat Intelligence Platform (TIP)  
Focus: Aggregate, correlate, automate threats

**cyber-pi + TQAKB =** Intelligence + Strategic Advisory  
Focus: Industry insights + actionable roadmaps + "what to buy"

**Critical Difference:** They provide data, we provide decisions

### Win Scenarios vs ThreatConnect

✅ Client wants industry-specific intelligence (not generic)  
✅ Client needs strategic guidance (not just threat feeds)  
✅ Client is budget-conscious ($5K vs $30K+)  
✅ Client values AI/semantic understanding  
✅ Client wants simple, focused reports (not complex platform)  
✅ Client lacks large analyst team (doesn't need platform)

### Lose Scenarios

❌ Client needs SOAR automation  
❌ Client wants community threat sharing  
❌ Client has 20+ person SOC team (needs platform)  
❌ Client already invested heavily in ThreatConnect  
❌ RFP mandates TIP platform architecture

### Sales Strategy vs ThreatConnect

**Positioning:**  
*"ThreatConnect is a powerful platform for large security teams. We're strategic intelligence for lean teams that need to know what to do, not just what's happening."*

**Lead With:**
1. Strategic advisory (they don't have this)
2. Industry specialization (18 deep verticals)
3. Simplicity (reports vs platform)
4. Cost (60% savings)

**Don't Lead With:**
- Source count (we lose: 80 vs 500)
- Platform features (we lose: they have SOAR)
- Community (we lose: they have large community)

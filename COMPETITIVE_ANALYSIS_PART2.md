❌ High maintenance overhead

- Anomali: Large SOC teams, technical analysts

- Anomali: Platform for threat analysis
- Us: Intelligence for strategic decisions

**Pricing:**
- Anomali: $50K-150K (3-6x our price)
- Us: $5K-25K

**Sales Approach:**  
*"Anomali is a powerful analysis platform for large teams. We're strategic intelligence for teams that need to act fast with limited resources."*

**Enables:**

- Threat actor campaign tracking
- IOC correlation
- Predictive analysis

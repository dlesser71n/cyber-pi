**Case Study 1: SolarWinds (2020, still relevant)**
- Supply chain compromise
- 18,000 customers affected (9 federal agencies confirmed)
- Dwell time: 9+ months
- Cost: Billions (response + rebuild)
- Lesson: Supply chain security, zero trust, assume breach

**Case Study 2: Colonial Pipeline (2021, still lessons)**
- Ransomware impact on critical infrastructure
- Not government but government response
- DarkSide ransomware, $4.4M paid
- Fuel shortages, state of emergency
- Lesson: OT security, backup procedures, public-private coordination

**Case Study 3: City of Atlanta (2018, recurring pattern)**
- SamSam ransomware, city services down
- Cost: $17M (recovery, no ransom paid)
- Recovery: Months
- Lesson: State/local cybersecurity funding critical
